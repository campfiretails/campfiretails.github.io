Campfire Tails 2016

August 4th - August 8th

Posted on September 25, 2015

This year

First, we’d like to thank everyone that attended Campfire Tails 2015, our biggest and best year yet.

We had 143 staff and attendees on site, and it was a blast.

Extra, special thanks to those of you who volunteered this year. We couldn’t have done it without you!

Next year

We’re still working on updating our site for next year, but we now have confirmed dates for Campfire Tails 2016: Thursday, August 4th to Monday, August 8th.

The theme is “Over The River and Through The Woods.”

We expect to open registration for Campfire Tails 2016 later this year.
