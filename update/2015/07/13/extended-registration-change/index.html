Change to Extended Registration

Attendee Registration will cost $70 during Extended Registration

Posted on July 13, 2015

Price Reduced

We’re reducing the extended registration price from $80 to $70, for a basic Attendee registration. The price increase (compared to $60 during general registration) is to pass on the added cost of buying things at the last minute, and we wanted to keep it in line with the actual cost to us.

Sponsor and Patron registration is not available during extended registration.

Extended Registration will still close on July 27th.

Reminder

There is no on-site registration for Campfire Tails.

You must register online before the close of Extended Registration (July 27th at 6 a.m.) in order to attend.
