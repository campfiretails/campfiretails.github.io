Registration Opens February 1st

Posted on January 3, 2016

Registration Schedule

Registration for Campfire Tails 2016 will open on February 1st at 6 a.m. Pacific time.

At that time, you will be able to register online at registration.campfiretails.org.

As always, you will need an account to register. You already have an account if you’ve used our Forum or attended Campfire Tails in the past. If you need any assistance, please contact registration@campfiretails.org.

Per our Registration Policy:

  • General registration for Campfire Tails 2016 ends on July 18th at 6:00 a.m. Pacific time.
  • Extended registration continues until August 1st at 6:00 a.m. Pacific time.
  • If we reach our attendance cap (170), registration closes immediately at that time.

Special Circumstances Policy

New this year, if you are in a special situation where you have no way of knowing if you can attend until during (or after) the extended registration period, we can arrange on a case-by-case basis a later go/no-go deadline for you.

Please see the Registration Policy for important details about this option.
