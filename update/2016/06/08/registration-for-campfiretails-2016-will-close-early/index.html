2016 Registration Will Close Early

The registration cap is fast approaching

Posted on June 8, 2016

Registration for Campfire Tails 2016 is going to hit the cap and close early this year.

Register at https://registration.campfiretails.org/

If you have not registered yet, but are planning on going, you need to complete your registration ASAP.

If you are hoping to see someone there, you need to encourage them to complete their registration ASAP if they haven’t already done so.

As always, there is no on-site registration. Once we hit the limit and the last outstanding / in-progress registrations are completed, your only option to attend will be if someone transfers their registration to you.
